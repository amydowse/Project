EDITING AN EXISTING PROCEDURE

Select a procedure from the list on the left hand side of the screen.

All of the information will then be displayed in the text boxes at the centre of the screen.

You can then edit this information.

Click into the area where you want to enter data, enter the data and click off of it. You can press the 'Tab' button on the keyboard to go to each section.

You must fill in all of the information.

When you have finished editing them, press the 'Save' button. If the save is successful the text boxes will be cleared.


DELETING AN EXISTING PROCEDURE

Select an existing procedure from the list on the left hand side of the screen.

All of the information will then be displayed in the text boxes at the centre of the screen.

You then press the 'Delete' button - you will be asked to confirm that you want to delete that procedure.

When you say 'Yes', their details are deleted and the text boxes are cleared.

If an appointment has been made for that procedure, that appointment will still be visible in the diary.


ADDING A NEW PROCEDURE

Click on the 'Add' button to bring up a new window.

You then fill in the procedures information - you must fill in all information.

You need to enter a number for the duration and number of nurses required, if you don't you will get an error message.

When finished, click the 'Save' button.

The window will close and the new procedure will appear in the main procedure list and will be available in making appointments.


Procedures where the location is 'Bed' can only have 1 nurse to 1 patient or 1 nurse to multiple patients or multiple nurses to 1 patient.

Procedures where the location is 'Non-bed' can only have 1 nurse to 1 patient.
